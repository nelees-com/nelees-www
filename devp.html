What Is Nelees One?

Nelees One is a modular, open-source smart ceiling platform. Think lighting, sensors, Bluetooth audio, environmental monitors, and more—integrated into a single ceiling-mounted unit. It's hexagonal, it's open, and it's designed to replace multiple different devices with one elegant system that lives out of your way but does everything.

What You'll Actually Do

  • Write firmware using ESP-IDF or Zephyr to power the modules
  • Design PCB layouts for new add-on modules (sensors, lights, audio, etc)
  • Build a 24V power distribution interface and modular connectors
  • Design enclosures that mount cleanly and securely on ceilings
  • Implement Matter integration and local-first smart home logic
  • Help define our SDK and documentation for future contributors

Why We're Doing This

Smart home products are a dumpster fire of closed ecosystems, annoying setup rituals, and data-grabbing nonsense. We're building Nelees One to be open, modular, and actually smart. Join us at the start.
