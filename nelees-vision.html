Our Vision

Nelees leads a global movement in the consumer electronics industry, championing open-source and open-hardware innovations created by people, for people. Nelees fosters a culture of ecological responsibility, collaboration, and forward-thinking innovation.

By challenging existing market practices that prioritize profit over people, we are shaping a new era where technology is fair, inclusive, and accessible to all. Our mission is to deliver a secure, safe, and comfortable life for everyone today and into the future.
