Vision

We're building a new kind of smart home company—one that puts users, developers, and integrators first. Nelees delivers modular, open-source hardware that’s designed to integrate with the systems people already use.
Our mission: make home automation more flexible, more private, and more future-proof. No lock-ins. No forced updates. No black boxes.

Open Technology for the Smart Home Era

Smart homes today are overengineered and underdelivering. We’re changing that. Nelees One is a ceiling-mounted smart hub that consolidates lighting, sensors, audio, and expansion ports into a single, clean unit. Built on the MATTER standard, it speaks the language of every major platform and integrates cleanly with Home Assistant, voice assistants, and AI platforms.

Problem to solve

The smart home market is cluttered with products that:
  • are closed-source and proprietary
  • require complex setups and have poor integration
  • can’t talk to each other
People want systems that just work—without handing over data or control. Builders want flexibility. Installers want speed. DIYers want reliability.

Solution: Nelees One

A ceiling-mounted device that does more than light up a room. A host for endless addons.

Base version includes:

  • Adjustable white lighting + RGB
  • Air quality (CO₂, VOC, humidity, temperature)
  • Built-in Bluetooth speaker

Future Add-ons (open end):

  • Wi-Fi AP (Ubiquiti ready)
  • IR / NIR blasters for sleep therapy, heating, automations
  • On-device AI + voice control
  • Multiroom audio (Sonos-compatible)
  • ... community choices

Business Model

Nelees operates under a dual structure:
  • Nelees (Community): Open-source ecosystem of contributors and collaborators.
  • Nelees Hardware (Company): Manufacturer and distributor of Nelees devices under a maker-friendly license.

Revenue Channels:

  • DTC hardware sales (DIY kits and prebuilt)
  • Licenses for OEM and white-labeling
  • Add-on modules, software tools, and pro support
