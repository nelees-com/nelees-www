Meet the Team

Nelees is built by a community of passionate technologists and builders from around the world. We're not just contributors — we're co-creators shaping the future of open-source smart environments.

Whether writing drivers, printing enclosures, optimizing power delivery, or organizing beta test groups — every member of the team plays a part in building the Nelees ecosystem.
